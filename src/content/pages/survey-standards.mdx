### queXML

- queXML is a simple XML schema for designing questionnaires.
- **queXML is compatible with the DDI standard for data description.**
- Does not include survey logic/filter optiosn
- Compatible with:
    - Lime Survey
    - 

### XLSForm/ODK XForms

- XLSForm is a form standard created to help simplify the authoring of forms in Excel (or other spreadsheet programs)
- Supported by ODK and Kobo Toolbox and others: https://xlsform.org/en/#tools-that-support-xlsforms
- https://getodk.github.io/xforms-spec/#introduction
