| XLSForm Type | Survey Concept | When to Use | Analysis | Visualization |
|--------------|----------------|-------------|----------|---------------|
| `select_one` | Single choice (nominal) | Mutually exclusive categories | Frequencies, χ² | Bar chart, pie |
| `select_multiple` | Multiple choice (nominal) | Non-exclusive options | Multiple response sets | Stacked bar |
| `integer` | Discrete numeric | Counts, ages | Mean, median, range | Histogram |
| `range` | Likert / ordinal | Attitudes, agreement | Median, mode | Diverging bar |
| `text` | Open-ended | Exploratory, quotes | Coding, NLP | Word cloud |
| `matrix` (group) | Battery | Related items on same scale | Factor analysis, Cronbach's α | Heatmap |
